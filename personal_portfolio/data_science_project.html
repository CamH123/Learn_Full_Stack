Thyroid Disease Detector

Background:

Thyroid disease refers to conditions that affect the thyroid gland, leading to issues with hormone production that can impact metabolism, energy, and overall health. Data science can help by analyzing medical data to improve diagnosis, predict disease progression, and personalize treatment plans.

Data Collect from UCI Collection of Diagnoses

Python Libraries used: Sci-kit Learn, PyTorch, Numpy, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn
